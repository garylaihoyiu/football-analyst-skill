# Novelte Robotics Brand Styling

## Overview

This skill encodes Novelte Robotics' official visual identity. Apply it whenever creating or styling artifacts that represent the Novelte brand — slides, documents, web pages, reports, or marketing collateral.

## Brand Colors

### Primary Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Primary Accent** | Novelte Orange | `#FCA311` | `252, 163, 17` | CTAs, highlights, logo mark, key accents |
| **Dark Background** | Charcoal Black | `#3A3838` | `58, 56, 56` | Body text on white, dark accents, card borders |
| **Pure Black** | Deep Black | `#000000` | `0, 0, 0` | High-contrast sections, footer bars, logo backdrop |
| **Primary Light** | White | `#FFFFFF` | `255, 255, 255` | **Primary background** for all slides, pages, and surfaces |

### Secondary Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Muted Text** | Light Gray | `#DCDDDD` | `220, 221, 221` | Subheadings, secondary text, dividers |
| **Warm Accent** | Amber | `#FF6B00` | `255, 107, 0` | Occasional highlight variant to Orange |
| **Dark Surface** | Dark Charcoal | `#1A1A1A` | `26, 26, 26` | Card backgrounds, section dividers |

### Color Usage Rules

- **White-first design**: Novelte's default background is white (`#FFFFFF`). Use white for all slide/page backgrounds, documents, and web surfaces.
- **Orange is precious**: Use `#FCA311` sparingly — for the most important element on the page (CTA button, key stat, logo mark accent, section headers). Never flood large areas with it.
- **Charcoal for text**: Use `#3A3838` for all body text and headings on white backgrounds — never pure black.
- **Dark sections for contrast**: Black/charcoal backgrounds are reserved for hero panels, closing slides, footer bars, and deliberate high-impact moments.
- **Never use orange on white without a dark text pairing** — always ensure sufficient contrast.

## Logo

### Versions
- **Dark version** (`dark.png`): Full wordmark in black with orange accent marks — for use on white/light backgrounds
- **White version** (`novelte_white.png`): Full wordmark in white with orange accent marks — for use on black/dark backgrounds

### Logo Mark
The "N" and "V" letterforms feature distinctive angular orange accent shapes (parallelogram cuts) — this geometric orange element is central to Novelte's visual identity and should be referenced in design motifs where appropriate.

### Logo Usage Rules
- Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the "N" character around the logo
- Never recolor, distort, rotate, or add effects to the logo
- Never place the dark logo on dark backgrounds — always use the white version
- Minimum size: 80px wide for digital, 25mm for print

## Typography

### Font Stack

| Role | Primary Font | Fallback |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Headings / Display** | Roboto Black | Arial Black, sans-serif |
| **Subheadings / Labels** | Roboto Bold | Arial Bold, sans-serif |
| **Body Text** | Roboto | Arial, sans-serif |
| **Captions / Fine Print** | Roboto Light | Arial, sans-serif |

> **Note**: Roboto is Novelte's system font as confirmed across all brand materials. It should be installed before use. Download from Google Fonts if not available: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Roboto

### Type Scale

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Color |
|---------|------|------|--------|-------|
| Hero/Display title | Roboto Black | 48–60pt | 900 | `#3A3838` or Orange on white; White on dark panels |
| Slide/Page title | Roboto Black | 36–44pt | 900 | `#3A3838` on white bg; White on dark bg |
| Section header | Roboto Bold | 24–28pt | 700 | `#FCA311` Orange or `#3A3838` on white |
| Body text | Roboto | 14–16pt | 400 | `#3A3838` on white bg; `#DCDDDD` on dark bg |
| Caption / label | Roboto Light | 10–12pt | 300 | `#888888` on white bg; `#DCDDDD` on dark bg |
| Tagline / subtitle | Roboto | 16–18pt | 400 | `#3A3838` on white bg; `#DCDDDD` on dark bg |

### Typography Rules
- **ALL CAPS** is acceptable and encouraged for product names, section labels, and navigation items — matches the logo's uppercase wordmark style
- Left-align body text; center only for titles and hero elements
- Generous letter-spacing (0.05–0.1em) for uppercase labels gives a premium, tech feel
- Avoid serif fonts entirely — Novelte is a clean, modern sans-serif brand

## Visual Design Principles

### Layout
- **White background dominant**: Default all slides, pages, and surfaces to white (`#FFFFFF`). White should occupy 60–70% of visual weight.
- **High contrast accents**: Use `#FCA311` orange and `#3A3838` charcoal to create focus and hierarchy against white.
- **Dark panels for impact**: Reserve black/charcoal full-bleed sections for hero covers, section dividers, and closing slides.
- **Geometric motifs**: Angular shapes, sharp edges, diagonal cuts — mirrors the logo's parallelogram accent.
- **Clean and spacious**: Generous whitespace; don't crowd elements.
- **Grid-based**: Align everything to a consistent grid; asymmetric layouts with purpose.

### Shapes & Graphic Elements
- Use **angular parallelogram shapes** (referencing the logo accent) as decorative dividers or highlights
- Thin **horizontal rules** in orange (`#FF6B00`) as section dividers
- **Rectangular image frames** — no rounded corners; sharp, precise edges match brand personality
- Avoid gradients unless very subtle dark-to-darker background treatments

### Photography & Imagery Style
- Product photography: clean, studio-lit robots on white or near-black backgrounds
- Lifestyle/environment shots: healthcare, hospitality, and logistics settings
- Color-grade photos with slight desaturation and a cool-neutral tone
- Never use warm-filter or highly saturated imagery

## Presentation Templates (PPTX)

### Slide Structure
- **Title / Cover slides**: White background, large `#3A3838` Roboto Black title, orange accent bar or mark; dark logo version
- **Content slides**: White background (`#FFFFFF`), `#3A3838` headings, `#3A3838` body text, orange for highlights/accents
- **Section dividers**: Black or charcoal full-bleed with large white/orange section title
- **Data/spec slides**: White background, orange for key numbers/stats (60–72pt), `#3A3838` labels below
- **Closing slide**: Dark (black/charcoal) background with white logo (white version), website URL in light gray

### Deck Conventions
- Slide size: **Widescreen 16:9** (13.33" × 7.50")
- Never use clip art, drop shadows, or decorative borders
- Product images should have no background (transparent PNG)
- Use thin orange horizontal lines to separate header from content when needed

## Document Templates

### Word / PDF Documents
- Cover page: White background, `#3A3838` Roboto Black title, orange accent bar at left margin
- Interior pages: White background, `#3A3838` body text, orange for section headings
- Headers/footers: Light gray rule, logo in top-right corner (dark version on white bg)
- Table styles: Orange header row (`#FF6B00`) with white text; alternating `#F5F5F5` / white rows

## Web / Digital

### CSS Variables
```css
:root {
  --color-primary:      #FCA311;   /* Novelte Orange */
  --color-bg-default:   #FFFFFF;   /* White — primary background */
  --color-bg-dark:      #000000;   /* Deep Black — hero/footer sections */
  --color-bg-surface:   #3A3838;   /* Charcoal — dark panels */
  --color-bg-card:      #F5F5F5;   /* Off-White — card backgrounds on white */
  --color-text-primary: #3A3838;   /* Charcoal — primary text on white */
  --color-text-muted:   #888888;   /* Mid Gray — captions on white */
  --color-accent-warm:  #FF6B00;   /* Amber */

  --font-display:  'Roboto Black', 'Arial Black', sans-serif;
  --font-heading:  'Roboto', 'Arial', sans-serif;
  --font-body:     'Roboto', 'Arial', sans-serif;

  --radius:        0px;            /* No rounding — sharp edges */
  --spacing-unit:  8px;
}
```

### Component Defaults
- **Buttons (primary)**: Orange background (`#FF6B00`), white text, Roboto Bold, no border radius, uppercase
- **Buttons (secondary)**: Transparent, `#3A3838` border (1px), charcoal text, hover → orange border + text
- **Cards**: White (`#FFFFFF`) background, 1px `#DCDDDD` border, `#3A3838` text
- **Navigation**: White background, charcoal links, orange active/hover state
- **Dividers**: `#DCDDDD` light gray or thin 1px orange rule
